About Ultimate Fitness

A serious training floor for serious progress.

Ultimate Fitness Gym is built for people who want measurable transformation. From beginners to advanced athletes, we provide structured programming, attentive coaching, and a disciplined environment designed to drive sustainable progress.

Located in Khidirpur, Kolkata, we focus on strength, conditioning, and body composition with a community-first atmosphere.

  • Elite-grade free weights and machines
  • Certified personal trainers and progress tracking
  • Focused fat-loss and muscle-building systems
  • High-energy culture and accountable coaching
